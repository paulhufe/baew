Bristol Applied Economics Meetings

Welcome!

The Bristol Applied Economics Meetings (BÆM) are a series of independent workshops that convene the best applied economists from Europe and beyond. Workshops are organised by the School of Economics at the University of Bristol and reflect the full breadth of applied economic research in Bristol.
